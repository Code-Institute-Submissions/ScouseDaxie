Why Dachshunds.

Sausage Dogs, Weiners, Daxies, Doxies.
What these pooches lack in height they make up for in the number of names they go by.
These cheeky hounds come in both Standard and Miniature variety, Three different coats and more colours than you'll find on a Lambanana.

The definition of loyal, stubbornly brave with an extra helping of confidence, Dachshunds are perfect city dwellers and extremely popular in the vibrant city of Liverpool.

Adorable doe-eyes, floppy ears, extra-long body and plucky attitude helped establish them at the top spot of the dog sofa.
