Why Dachshunds.

Sausage Dogs, Weiners, Daxies, Doxies.
What these pooches lack in height they make up for in the number of names they go by.
These cheeky hounds come in both Standard and Minature variety, Three different coats and more colours than you'll find on a Lambanana.

The definition of loyal, except seemingly born with selective hearing and without a true appreciation for their diminuative stature, Dachshunds are perfect city dwellers and extremely popular in the vibrant city of Liverpool.

Adorable doe-eyes, floppy ears, extra-long body and plucky attitude helped establish them at the top spot of the dog sofa.
